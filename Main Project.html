Top Researchers

Prominent experts in this fields.

Mark Daly

Mark Daly is the founding chief of the Analytic and Translational Genetics Unit (ATGU) at Massachusetts General Hospital and an assistant professor at the Harvard Medical School. His research has historically focused on the development and application of statistical methods for the discovery and interpretation of genetic variation responsible for complex human disease.

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Bert Vogelstein

Dr. Vogelstein is the Clayton Professor of Oncology and Pathology and co-director of the Ludwig Center for Cancer Genetics and Therapeutics at the Sidney Kimmel Comprehensive Cancer Center of Johns Hopkins University School of Medicine.

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Eric Steven Lander

Eric S Lander is a core institute member and founding director of the Broad Institute of MIT and Harvard. He served as president and founding director from Broad's inception until 2021. A geneticist, molecular biologist, and mathematician, he has played a pioneering role in all aspects of the reading, understanding, and biomedical application of the human genome. He was a principal leader of the international Human Genome Project.

Top Medical Institutions

Prestigious hospitals and clinics in this domain.

New York Genome Center

New York Genome Center (NYGC) is a non-profit research institution located in New York City. Its primary mission is to advance genomic research and develop cutting-edge technologies to better understand human genetics and its implications for health and disease.

Dana-Farber

Dana-Farber is dedicated to providing expert, compassionate care to cancer patients while conducting groundbreaking research to advance our understanding of cancer and develop innovative treatments.

Howard Hughes Medical Institute

Howard Hughes Medical Institute (HHMI) is a leading nonprofit organization dedicated to advancing biomedical research and science education. HHMI supports exceptional scientists and promotes science education initiatives at all levels.
